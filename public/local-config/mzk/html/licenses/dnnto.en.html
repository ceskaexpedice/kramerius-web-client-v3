What are out-of-commerce works (DNNT) and how can I access the document?

Out of Commerce Works (DNNT) are works that are still protected by copyright law, but they are not available commercially on the market anymore. As such, they are included in the List of out-of-commerce works kept by the National Library of the Czech Republic. Under the DNNT-licence, registered users may access, from a site in the Czech Republic, monographs published up to 2004 and periodicals published until 2014.

If you do not have login details, you can register for the service at one of the participating institutions. The list of such institutions can be seen here.

DNNT are available to the registered users on the basis of licence agreements between National Library of the Czech Republic and the collective rights management organisations DILIA and OOA-S.

If you cannot access the document under licenses, then the general rules for non-public documents apply
