Information on the Processing of Personal Data when using the services of the National Digital Library - Out-Of-Commerce Works

General information, contact details of the controller and an overview of the rights of the data subject and their exercise are available on the controller's website: https://www.mzk.cz/zpracovani-osobnich-udaju.

Specific information on the above activities:

  • Description of Activity: Providing previews/scans of works from the NDK-DNNT database available to users on the basis of a contractual license within the framework of extended collective management pursuant to § 97f of Act No. 121/2000 Coll., the Copyright Act, as amended, through the Kramerius information system
  • Position: Controller / Processor
  • Purposes of Processing: Provision of library and information services to the general public, support of education and culture
  • Legal basis for Processing: Article 6(1)(a) GDPR - consent of the data subject, Article 6(1)(b) GDPR - performance of a contract, in some cases Article 6(1)(c) GDPR - compliance with a legal obligation, Article 6(1)(f) GDPR - legitimate interest of the Controller
  • Description of Categories of Data Subjects: third parties, registered users
  • Processed Personal Data: e-mail address, telephone number, address, eduID, username (pseudonym), affiliation to an institution (organization ID), date and time of authentication, session ID, IP address, pages viewed
  • Data Retention Period: max. 1 calendar year after revocation of access authorisation
  • Personal data are subject to automated processing but not profiling
